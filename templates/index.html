About

Who we are...

Melksham Comic Con is a non-profit event that celebrates and promotes comic books and sequential art in its many forms, from web comics, independent works, publishers, printers, retailers and so much more!

Who we're for...

We’re the perfect convention for people who have never been to one, or want to see what it’s all about.

Where are we?

Taking place in the heart of Wiltshire, surrounded by beautiful countryside! Locate us here.
